What is mass?

Mass is typically defined as the amount of matter within an object.It is most commonly measured as inertial mass ,involving an object's resistance to acceleration given some net force. Matter,however,is somewhat loosely defined in science,and cannont be precisely measured.In classical physics, matter is any substance that has mass and volume.
